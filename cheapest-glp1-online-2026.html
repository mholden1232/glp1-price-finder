Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest GLP-1 medication in 2026?

GobyMeds offers the lowest entry price at $99/month for a 3-month compounded semaglutide starter bundle. For long-term treatment at therapeutic doses, Eden Health and MyStart Health's price lock guarantees often result in lower total costs. NovoCare's direct pharmacy offers brand-name Wegovy starting at $149/month for new patients.

Is compounded semaglutide safe?

Compounded semaglutide is prepared by licensed U.S. 503A compounding pharmacies and is widely prescribed through legitimate telehealth providers. It is not FDA-approved, which means it has not undergone FDA review for safety, effectiveness, or manufacturing quality. The FDA has issued warning letters to specific providers for violations — not to all compounding providers. Choosing a provider that works with PCAB-accredited or otherwise verified pharmacies reduces risk.

Can I use HSA or FSA for GLP-1 medications?

Yes. GLP-1 medications prescribed by a licensed provider for weight management or diabetes are qualified medical expenses under HSA and FSA rules. All providers listed on our site accept HSA and FSA cards. This effectively reduces your cost by your marginal tax rate — typically 22 to 37% for working adults.

What happened to cheap compounded GLP-1 after the FDA shortage ruling?

The FDA determined in February 2025 that the semaglutide shortage was resolved, which affected some 503B bulk compounding operations. However, patient-specific 503A compounding by licensed pharmacies with valid prescriptions remains legal. Most reputable telehealth platforms transitioned their pharmacy relationships accordingly and continue operating. Prices have largely held steady through this transition.

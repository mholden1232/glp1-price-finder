What is Wellorithm?

Wellorithm is a telehealth platform offering compounded GLP-1 medications — primarily semaglutide and tirzepatide — for weight management. The platform is designed around a technology-first model that connects patients with licensed healthcare professionals for evaluation, prescription, and ongoing care.

One of Wellorithm's most notable features is its performance-based guarantee. Patients who do not lose at least 10% of their baseline weight after 16 weeks of medication adherence may be eligible for a refund of up to four months of program fees. This type of guarantee is rare in the GLP-1 telehealth space.

✓ Wellorithm's key differentiator

The 10% weight loss guarantee is one of the few performance-based promises in the GLP-1 telehealth market. Eligibility conditions apply and must be verified directly with Wellorithm, but the offer signals confidence in their program outcomes.

How Wellorithm Works

Step 1: Online intake questionnaire

Complete a health questionnaire covering your medical history, current medications, and weight loss goals. The intake screens for eligibility and determines whether GLP-1 medication is appropriate for your situation.

Step 2: Licensed provider evaluation

A licensed healthcare professional reviews your intake. Wellorithm notes that patients with complex comorbidities or insulin-dependent diabetes may be better served by a specialist who can manage their full health picture.

Step 3: Medication delivery

If approved, your prescription is sent to a licensed compounding pharmacy and medication ships directly to your door. Wellorithm works with state-licensed 503A compounding pharmacies.

Step 4: Ongoing monitoring

Access 24/7 support through phone at (877) 402-6778 or email at contact@wellorithm.com for questions and care coordination.

The 10% Weight Loss Guarantee

Wellorithm's performance guarantee states that patients who do not lose at least 10% of their baseline weight after 16 weeks may be eligible for a refund of up to four months of program fees. Always confirm the exact current terms directly with Wellorithm before enrolling if the guarantee is a deciding factor in your choice.

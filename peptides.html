💡 What most people don't realize

Ozempic, Wegovy, Zepbound, and Mounjaro are all peptides — synthetic versions of naturally occurring gut hormones. The peptides on this page work through different mechanisms but share the same goal: helping your body regulate metabolism, reduce fat, and support weight loss. GLP-1s have the strongest clinical evidence. Other peptides are increasingly popular as adjuncts or alternatives.

Tier 3 — GLP-1 Alternatives & Fat-Specific Peptides
AOD-9604 & MOTS-c

For patients who cannot tolerate GLP-1s or want additional fat-targeting support. These peptides work through different mechanisms — targeting fat metabolism directly rather than appetite.

🔥
AOD-9604
Anti-Obesity Drug fragment · HGH fragment 176-191
Fat-specific mechanism No blood sugar effects Research compound

AOD-9604 is derived from a specific fragment of the human growth hormone molecule associated with fat metabolism. Unlike GLP-1s which work primarily through appetite suppression, AOD-9604 targets fat directly — stimulating lipolysis (fat breakdown) and inhibiting lipogenesis (new fat creation) without significantly affecting blood sugar or insulin. This makes it a potential option for patients who want fat loss support without GLP-1 side effects.

How it differs from GLP-1s
  • No appetite suppression mechanism
  • No blood sugar or insulin effects
  • Targets fat metabolism directly
  • No GI side effects typical of GLP-1s
Limitations
  • Much less clinical evidence than GLP-1s
  • Not FDA-approved for weight loss
  • Results less dramatic than semaglutide
  • Often stacked with other peptides
Best as a complement to GLP-1 therapy or for GLP-1 intolerant patients

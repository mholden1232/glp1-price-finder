What is GobyMeds?

GobyMeds is an online telehealth platform that connects patients with licensed U.S. healthcare providers for compounded GLP-1 weight loss medications — primarily semaglutide and tirzepatide. Founded as a direct-to-consumer platform, GobyMeds has built a reputation for transparent pricing and a streamlined intake process.

The platform is fully virtual. You complete a health questionnaire online, a licensed provider reviews your case, and if approved, medication ships directly to your door from a licensed compounding pharmacy. No office visits required.

💡 What is compounded GLP-1?

Compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide are custom-prepared versions of the same active ingredients found in Wegovy, Ozempic, Zepbound, and Mounjaro. They are made by licensed 503A compounding pharmacies and are significantly cheaper than brand-name versions. They are not FDA-approved but are legal and widely prescribed through telehealth platforms.

How GobyMeds Works

Step 1: Complete the online intake

You answer a health questionnaire covering your medical history, current medications, weight loss goals, and eligibility. The platform asks real screening questions — not just basic contact info — and shows plan options before the final payment step.

Step 2: Provider review

A licensed healthcare provider reviews your intake. No video consultation is required unless your state law mandates it. If approved, a prescription is written and sent to a compounding pharmacy partner.

Step 3: Medication ships to your door

GobyMeds partners with 503A and 503B licensed compounding pharmacies. Medication ships overnight with cold-pack packaging at no extra charge. Specific pharmacy names are not disclosed publicly but can be requested after enrollment.

Step 4: Ongoing management

Use the self-serve patient portal to manage refills, track orders, and communicate with your care team. Dose increases are managed through the platform as you progress through treatment.

What Real Patients Say

GobyMeds has consistently positive reviews across Reddit communities including r/Semaglutide and r/GLP1Agonists. Common themes in positive reviews include fast shipping (often 2 to 3 days), a simple intake process, and appreciation for transparent pricing.

Critical feedback tends to focus on the significant price increase after the starter bundle ends, and the lack of real-time support for patients who have questions during treatment. Some users have also noted delays during high-demand periods.

The platform holds LegitScript certification, which is a meaningful third-party verification that GobyMeds meets regulatory and safety standards for online pharmacies and telehealth platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GobyMeds legit?

Yes. GobyMeds is LegitScript certified, which means it has been verified by a third-party organization that reviews online pharmacies and telehealth platforms for compliance with legal and safety standards. Medications are dispensed by licensed 503A and 503B compounding pharmacies.

Does GobyMeds accept insurance?

No. GobyMeds does not accept insurance for compounded medications. However, they do accept HSA and FSA cards with a MasterCard logo. Brand-name options like Wegovy and Zepbound may be available for patients with insurance coverage, though availability varies.

What states does GobyMeds serve?

GobyMeds is available in all 50 states via telehealth. However, eligibility for compounded medications specifically may vary by state due to state-specific pharmacy regulations.

Does GobyMeds require lab work?

No. GobyMeds does not routinely require lab work before prescribing. This makes the onboarding process faster but removes a safety checkpoint that most obesity specialists recommend. If you have not had recent bloodwork, consider sharing results from your primary care provider before starting treatment.

Can I get a refund if I am not approved?

Yes. GobyMeds offers a full refund if your prescription is not approved. Once a prescription is written and medication ships, refunds are no longer available.

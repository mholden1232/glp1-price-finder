What is SkinnyRx?

SkinnyRx is a telehealth platform operated by Lean Rx, Inc. out of Sacramento, California. It connects patients with licensed U.S. healthcare providers who evaluate and prescribe compounded GLP-1 medications for weight loss. The platform offers both semaglutide and tirzepatide in multiple formats — which is its most notable differentiator in an increasingly crowded market.

Most GLP-1 telehealth platforms offer only weekly injectable medications. SkinnyRx gives patients three administration options, making it one of the few platforms where needle-averse patients can access compounded GLP-1 therapy in a format they are actually comfortable with.

How SkinnyRx Works

Step 1: Complete the online intake

Spend 5 to 10 minutes completing a health questionnaire covering your medical history, current medications, weight loss goals, and any contraindications. The form screens for GLP-1 eligibility upfront.

Step 2: Provider review

A licensed physician reviews your intake and determines the appropriate medication and format. Most reviews are completed within 24 to 48 hours. You receive a personalized treatment plan with a titration schedule. No video appointment is required in most states.

Step 3: Choose your format

Select injectable, sublingual, or tablet based on your preference and provider recommendation. Your prescription is sent to SkinnyRx's partner compounding pharmacy, prepared at your prescribed dosage, and ships overnight with cold-pack packaging for injectables.

Step 4: Ongoing refills

Monthly refills are processed automatically. You must cancel before processing to avoid being charged for the next shipment. Once medication ships, refunds are not available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SkinnyRx legitimate?

Yes. SkinnyRx is operated by Lean Rx, Inc., is LegitScript certified, and works with licensed U.S. physicians and state-licensed compounding pharmacies. Its 4.8 Trustpilot rating from thousands of verified customers supports its legitimacy. The BBB complaints relate primarily to billing and cancellation practices, not medication safety or clinical quality.

What is the difference between injectable, sublingual, and tablet GLP-1?

Injectable semaglutide is a weekly subcutaneous injection — the same delivery method as Ozempic and Wegovy. Sublingual drops are a daily liquid placed under the tongue and absorbed through the mucosa — no needles required. Oral tablets are swallowed daily and are the most convenient format for patients who prefer not to self-inject. All three contain the same active ingredients but differ in delivery method, absorption rate, and daily versus weekly dosing.

Can I cancel SkinnyRx easily?

This is the most important question to answer before enrolling. SkinnyRx's cancellation process requires a phone call — you cannot cancel through the patient portal alone. Once a shipment is processed, refunds are not available. Cancel before your next billing date to avoid charges for the following month. Multiple BBB complaints relate to this process, so set a calendar reminder for your billing date if you enroll.

Does SkinnyRx accept insurance?

No. SkinnyRx is cash-pay only. HSA and FSA cards are accepted.

How long does shipping take?

SkinnyRx offers free overnight shipping on all orders. Most patients report receiving medication within 2 to 3 days of approval. Some users report longer waits during high-demand periods.

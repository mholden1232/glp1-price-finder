How to Choose the Right GLP-1 Provider

The right provider depends entirely on your situation. Here is a simple framework:

  • If you have insurance that might cover GLP-1s — start with Found Health. They will check your coverage for free and navigate prior authorization on your behalf.
  • If you are paying cash and want the lowest entry price — GobyMeds at $99/month is the clear choice for the starter bundle.
  • If you plan to be on GLP-1 therapy for 6 to 12 months — Eden Health's price lock may save you more money overall despite the higher starting price.
  • If you want clinical monitoring with labs included — MyStart Health is one of the few budget options that includes blood work.
  • If you want a performance guarantee — Wellorithm's 10% weight loss guarantee is unique in this market.
⚠️ What to watch out for

Always ask about pricing at higher doses before enrolling. Many providers advertise a low entry price but charge significantly more once you reach therapeutic doses after 3 to 4 months. Eden Health and MyStart Health both offer price lock guarantees that protect you from this. GobyMeds and most others do not.

What is Eden Health?

Eden is a telehealth platform offering GLP-1 medications for weight loss, including both compounded and brand-name options like Wegovy and Zepbound. With over 50,000 members served, Eden is one of the more established players in the compounded GLP-1 telehealth space.

What sets Eden apart is its same-price-at-every-dose guarantee. Most GLP-1 telehealth platforms increase your monthly cost as your dosage goes up. Eden locks in your price from day one, making it much easier to budget for long-term treatment.

✓ Eden's key differentiator

Eden uses PCAB-accredited compounding pharmacies with third-party tested formulations. This is a higher pharmacy quality standard than most telehealth platforms require, and is one of the clearest reasons to choose Eden over cheaper alternatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Eden Health legitimate?

Yes. Eden works with board-certified physicians and PCAB-accredited compounding pharmacies. The company has served over 50,000 members and maintains transparent pricing on its website.

Does Eden accept insurance?

Eden does not accept insurance for compounded medications. HSA and FSA cards are accepted. Brand-name options may be available through insurance depending on your plan.

What is the same-price guarantee?

Your monthly cost does not increase as your dosage goes up during treatment. This is a meaningful differentiator because most GLP-1 telehealth platforms charge more at higher doses, which can double or triple your monthly cost over 6 to 12 months.

Does Eden require lab work?

Lab work is at the provider's discretion and is not routinely required. If you have underlying conditions, your provider may request labs before prescribing.
